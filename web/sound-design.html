About

McBride Sound Design is a full-service, audio post-production studio that provides completely custom and totally unique soundtracks for a variety of media including Film, TV, Advertising, Gaming and Physical Environments.

  • - Ryan in the Vermont Studio
  • - A typical Sound Design Session

  • WHO WE ARE

    McBride Sound Design is headed by Ryan McBride whose credits include work for nearly every major film studio and numerous acclaimed games, recording artists and physical attractions. Ryan's diverse background and education brings a deep understanding of sound synthesis, engineering and musical composition.

  • CUSTOM EVERYTHING

    With the proliferation of high quality sound libraries available for purchase these days, it's typical to encounter the usual suspects in many productions (that feeling of 'haven't I heard this before?'). McBride Sound Design's process is rooted in the creation of completely original material that creates lasting impressions. Through various recording and synthesis techniques, McBride exceeds expectations elevating the impact of the end product.

CLIENTS

McBride Sound Design's clients represent some of the biggest, most respected names in the industry. We strive to bring the level of professionalism and quality typically associated with these brands to all of the work we do.

  • SOUND DESIGN

    We create custom audio atmospheres and iconic sounds from scratch across a variety of media who's ultimate objective is to illicit an emotional response. Our sound design is the process of transparent research, experimentation and technicality who's final product is always high definition, fresh and highly effective.

  • AUDIO POST PRODUCTION

    We provide complete audio post production services for projects both large and small and are fully expandable by partnering with some of the most renowned specialty audio experts in the industry. This includes sound supervision, editorial, dialogue, foley and mix services for both film and television projects.

  • VOICEOVER AND ADR

    Most times, finding the perfect type of voice and voice actor is the most difficult part of a voiceover project. We maintain relationships with numerous talent agencies and VO artists worldwide that ensure we're always getting the appropriate fit. We provide complete voiceover and ADR casting, recording and editorial services.

  • RECORDING AND MIXING

    We maintain a fully equipped Recording Studio with field recording and foley capabilities (our studio is in the woods so it's pretty quiet!). Additionally, we provide full mixing services for both music and post production audio across a variety of formats and deliverables.

  • ORIGINAL MUSIC

    We provide complete music composition, scoring and orchestration services across a variety of genres. Our catalog includes everything from symphonies with performances by internationally recognized orchestras to hip hop and electronic beats for major recording artists. Whether it's a film score, beat programming or incidental music, we can provide a high level of custom musical composition.

  • SONIC BRANDING

    Sonic branding is the creation of a short but very distinctive sequence of sound that gives an identifiable sonic identity to a brand or piece of hardware. We've created sonic identities for numerous major brands and take cues from our diverse skill set of music, sound design and physical environmental soundscaping.

  • ENVIRONMENTAL SOUNDSCAPING

    Working closely with the industries top systems providers, we create compelling soundscapes for physical environments and have done so successfully for projects ranging from casinos and museums to restaurants and entertainment attractions. Through the integration of music and atmospheric sound effects properly mixed onsite, the guest is immersed into the experience.
